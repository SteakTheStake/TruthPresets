WELCOME TO TRUTH PRESETS

Description:

"Truth Presets" is ReShade Presets for Grand Theft Auto 5. We create Presets for FiveM and try our hardest in making them. We make presets for QuantV, NVE & StryVision with hopes for more in the future. We started this project with our FREE Presets pack for QuantV & then moved to NVE, later starting with StryVision. The future of "Truth Presets" is bright and we always welcome new members to join & experience our presets. Additionally, we create Custom ReShade Presets through our Patreon, so feel free to contact our team for more information.

Key Features:

  • Realistic and immersive visuals
  • Dynamic and lifelike lighting
  • Enhanced color correction

Why Choose "Truth Presets"?

Truth Presets is the result of countless hours of dedication and attention to detail. We've been creating ReShade Presets for Grand Theft Auto 5 for approximately a year. From our FREE Preset pack to our premium offers, we've always been passionate about producing presets and seeing people enjoy them.

Installation Instructions:

  1. Download one of our Presets, either the FREE ones or the premium ones.
  2. Once you have the Ini file, place it in your plugins folder.
  3. To stay organized, consider creating a folder named "Preset" and placing your presets inside.
  4. Enjoy our ReShade Presets!

Requirements:

  • Reshade (Version 5.9.2 or higher)
  • Grand Theft Auto 5 Or FiveM
